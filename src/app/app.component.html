Thiago Siqueira

Software Engineer, Husband, Dad, Scuba Diver

I'm a Consulting Manager at Prudential. My team is responsible for building solutions across Prudential's landscape and providing a better user experience to millions of customers.

Prior to that, I spent a few years in Moody's Analytics where I started as a Software Engineer and eventually led a team of engineers as an Associate Director - Software Engineer Manager. At Moody's Analytics, I worked on CreditEdge, a credit risk tool used mainly to support credit analysis.

Work aside, I'm a husband and father of two beautiful kids, and whenever I can, I try to be underwater or shooting some paint. I am a PADI certified Rescue Scuba Diver with (currently) over 40 dives. There's nothing quite like the experience of being completely underwater, in a place where it is pretty obvious we are just visitors. My other hobby of choice is Paintball, where the adrenaline and physical nature of the sport drew me in years ago.

After family and hobbies, I like to spend time listening to podcasts, watching training videos, or playing with new technology to learn new things and stay current with today's fast moving technology. There are two quotes I believe in immensely:
